GENETIC ENGINEERING



CONTROVERSY and ETHICAL ISSUES

Despite its many benefits, Genetic Engineering also comes with many issues such as but not limited to:

  • Humans Playing As God -- Since human beings are not omniscient, omnipotent, or all good, they should not assume the risk-laden responsibility that is inextricably tied up with many forms of genetic engineering.
  • Patients Being A Contentious Issue -- Contentious, as defined by Merriam Webster, is a being that is prone to cause disagreement or argument.
  • Introduction Of Dangerous Traits Into Microorganisms That Were Previously Free Of Them
  • As of the moment, regulations on Genetic Engineering are lacking, and thus, there is nothing saying right now to how far humans can go to playing as God.
